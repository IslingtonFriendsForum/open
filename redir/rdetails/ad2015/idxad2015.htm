ABOUT ARDUINO DAY

Arduino Day is a worldwide celebration of Arduino’s 11th birthday on 28 March 2015. 24 hours of global events
During this friendly and informal event we like to highlight how maker community create projects and collaborate on DIY electronics. If you’re interested in giving a demo of your project please get in touch.
Robots, drones and people welcome!
